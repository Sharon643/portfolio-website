About

I'm an AI/ML engineer in training, currently completing my Master's in Computer Applications at CUSAT. My interest is in the space where research meets deployment — building systems that actually run, not just notebooks that almost work.

My projects share a pattern: take a messy real-world problem (predict a tournament, evaluate a resume, keep a driver awake), pick the right model stack, and build the end-to-end pipeline including the UI. I'm drawn to GenAI and RAG right now but comfortable all the way down to classical ML when that's what the problem calls for.

Outside code I'm fluent in English and Malayalam, and I think Kerala is a quietly good place to be building things in AI right now.
